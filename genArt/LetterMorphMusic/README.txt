LetterMorphMusic - James Blair

1. Type desired words in top right input field and click add to begin visualization.
Once there are two words the letter animation will begin along to peaks in the music

2.Use gui to customize color

3. sensitivity is how high or low the amplitude must reach to trigger animation

4. When controlling speed be aware that the animation will not reset until current animation is complete

5. num points controls the density of the splines

6. adjust speed and sensitivity to find correct rhythm for your selected mp3

7. currently .ttf font are not supported as the position data is translated incorrectly
